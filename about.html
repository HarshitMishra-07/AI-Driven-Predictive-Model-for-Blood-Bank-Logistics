About Us

Blood Hive connects blood donors with recipients, streamlining the donation and distribution process to save lives. Our goal is to ensure a safe, reliable, and accessible blood supply by efficiently managing donor information, blood stock levels, and urgent requests.

Our Mission

We aim to prevent blood shortages and support healthcare providers with an organized, transparent system that simplifies donation and inventory management.
