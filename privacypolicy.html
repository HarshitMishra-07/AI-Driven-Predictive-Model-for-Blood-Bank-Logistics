Privacy & Policy

Effective Date: 1 January 2025

At Blood Hive, we value your trust and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your data.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you use our services:

  • Personal Information: Name, age, gender, contact details (email and phone number).
  • Health Information: Blood type, medical history related to donations.
  • Donation Details: Dates of donations, location, and frequency.
  • Usage Data: Information on how you interact with our platform.

2. How We Use Your Information

The data collected is used to:

  • Maintain accurate donor and recipient records.
  • Ensure eligibility for blood donation.
  • Facilitate communication for donation requests or updates.
  • Improve the functionality of the system.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share your personal information with third parties except in the following cases:

  • With Consent: When you provide explicit consent.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with laws or respond to legal requests.
  • Emergency Situations: To ensure public health and safety.

4. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of sensitive data.
  • Restricted access to authorized personnel only.
  • Regular system audits to identify vulnerabilities.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We may use cookies to enhance your experience on our platform. These cookies help us:

  • Analyze system usage.
  • Improve user navigation and system responsiveness.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings if preferred.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information.
  • Request corrections to your data.
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations).

7. Third-Party Links

Our system may include links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these websites. Please review their privacy policies before sharing your information.
